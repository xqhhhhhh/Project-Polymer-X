ExxonMobil HD 5453HL High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Copolymer Film Grade
Categories: Polymer; Film; Thermoplastic; Polyethylene (PE); High Density (HDPE); High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Copolymer Film Grade
Material Notes: ExxonMobil™ HD 5453HL is a high molecular weight, high-density polyethylene copolymer. This resin has superior stress crack resistance, high impact strength and good rigidity.

Availability:  Latin America, North America. 

Additive: Antistatic: No

Applications: drums, heavy gauge sheet, thermoformed parts, food packaging, large part blow molding.  Forms:  pellets.

Information provided by ExxonMobil.
Key Words: Paxon™ BA53-055
